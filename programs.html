Our Programs

Planning, Work & Life Balance

Based on a sociodemographic profile per employee, we develop policies and activities that allow us to improve the work and personal environment. These activities are aimed at satisfying the current needs, dreams or desires of the employees so that in this way they feel the commitment that the organization has with them. Learn about the different reward systems that organizations implement to motivate employees and impact their productivity, including monetary, non-monetary, and employee recognition.

Personal Achievement - Motivation

Although motivation is intrinsic and "no one motivates anyone" Our responsibility is to create motivating factors and generate favorable environments for the employee to find their own motivation and with it the strength to positively impact their performance, in such a way that performance contributes to the achievement of corporate objectives. Each individual is motivated by different things, and that is why employee motivation programs offer various incentive systems.

Time Management and Communication

We know the importance of training employees and developing their soft skills, but we are also aware of the cost that this can represent for an SME. Taking this information into account, we allied ourselves with a company that offers international training and we designed the Development Program for SMEs, a proposal based on the collaborative economy scheme!.

Senses Consultant


We are a Human Management consulting firm aware that the challenges faced by small and medeium bisinesses in terms of their most valuable resource, people, are very similar to those of large companies. However, their resources, maturity and vision of the environment do not allow them to choose to undertake the same solutions.

Mission


Bringing small and medeium bisinesses closer to integrating solutions from a human management department that understands that its work goes beyond hiring, paying social benefits, payroll and finalizing contracts.
