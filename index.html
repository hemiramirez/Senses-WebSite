Comprehensive Consultation In Human Management

We are a Human Management consulting firm aware that the challenges faced by our daily life in terms of their most valuable resource, people, are very similar to those of large companies. However, their resources, maturity and vision of the environment do not allow them to choose to undertake the same solutions. Through a diagnosis of the current processes of the organization, we measure, analyse, and intervene the organizational gaps that allow us to determine how aligned the organizational structure, workload, functions, and competencies profile of the collaborators are with the vision of the organization The Wellness Workshop is your trusted provider of highly engaging, evidence-based workplace wellbeing programs and workshops delivering both virtual and face-to-face formats across Australia and New Zealand on a range of health and wellness topics. We work with local and national businesses, schools, community organisations and government councils to enhance mental health, reduce stress, increase resilience, boost productivity, maximise performance and improve overall wellbeing. All programs are designed using evidence-based approaches by a range of qualified Allied Health Practitioners, Coaches and Trainers. All programs include custom designed promotional posters and tools to maximise participation within your organisation, activities that encourage instant action, online and over the phone support, and graphically designed summary sheets of your selected topic.
